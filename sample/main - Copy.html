About me

Life is not all about compromising. At times you have to stand for what you believe is your passion and take that calculated risk to make it happen. I Stared sharpening my pencils when I was a kid. From drawing sketches to painting posters, making handwritten magazines, I have done everything during my school days. My interest for digital art and photoshop designs increased during my engineering days. As my love for creation was always there, I started learning web engineering and have developed a few skills and accomplished some projects. Inspite of working in a business environment at Amazon, I have always tried to find places to use my skills to create something refreshing and innovative. At Amazon I have worked on an internal e-magazine, designed a few tools and made some officical trackers.
